About the MCCO

The Mani-Cretin Collaboration Organization is a multi-galactic organization aimed at carrying out the Mani-Cretin Collaboration Initiative on an universal scale, strengthening cooperation and collaboration between Giberia Republic of Mani Federation and the Cretin Republic of Federation B-101. Since its founding in 2022, it has been the leading multi-galactic organization within this universe.

Projects of the MCCO

Projects carried out by the MCCO are a key component of the Mani-Cretin Collaboration Initiative. All collaboration projects between the Giberia Republic and Cretin Republic are regulated by the MCCO, the umbrella organization for all such projects. MCCO projects are official government projects directly related to the Mani-Cretin Collaboration Initiative.
